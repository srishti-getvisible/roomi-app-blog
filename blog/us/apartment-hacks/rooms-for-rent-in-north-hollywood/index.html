Where is North Hollywood?

North Hollywood is located in the San Fernando Valley region of Los Angeles, California. It’s bordered by Burbank to the west, Studio City and Valley Village to the north, Sun Valley to the east, and Toluca Lake to the south. It’s a great place for those craving urban living at an affordable price. North Hollywood is the 55th most walkable neighborhood in Los Angeles. Even though it has a walk score of 66, it is a perfect place for people who love to get their steps in! Some errands can be run on foot, but you will mostly need a car or some other mode of transport to get things done.

Restaurants in North Hollywood

It’s no secret that LA is one of the biggest restaurant hubs in the US. In fact, restaurants in North Hollywood are proof of this! Restaurants in North Hollywood are known for their amazing food and unique ambiance. It’s no surprise that the eateries in this neighborhood draw a crowd – here are some you MUST try!

Parks in North Hollywood

  • Valley Village Park: If you like the idea of a weekend picnic with your friends then you can take a trip to Valley Village Park. The park is clean and well maintained and it includes a playground, picnic tables, and a multi-purpose field.
  • North Hollywood Recreation Center: This place will surely become one of your favorite hangout spots thanks to the huge range of activities on offer. The center is home to various sports fields, a gym, courts, and an outdoor pool as well.

The diverse drinks menu features a selection of beers on tap as well as a variety of signature cocktails from the bar staff.
