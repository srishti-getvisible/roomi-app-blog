Where is Culver City?

Named after its founder, Harry Culver, this Los Angeles County neighborhood is nestled between Downtown Los Angeles and Santa Monica. This makes it very convenient to commute to different areas of the city from. The neighborhood is adjacent to Beverly Hills to the north and Inglewood to the south. Around 40% of its buildings are single, detached homes, including large and small apartments.

Public Transit in Culver City, California

1. Walkability

With an average Walk Score of 75, Culver City is very walkable, and most daily errands can be accomplished on foot. Also, its residents enjoy convenient proximity to Los Angeles.

2. Roads and Traffic

As mentioned above, Culver City is close to Los Angeles, meaning that it won’t be so easy to avoid getting stuck in traffic. It’s advisable to plan long trips well in advance, or take the bus to be on the safe side.

3. Nearby Bus Stops

On Culver City’s bus service website, you can find many bus stops and bus routes close to you. You can view maps and bus schedules, get ETAs, and more. Find out more here: Culver CityBus.

4. Nearby Train Stations

Culver City is home to an elevated light rail station on the Los Angeles Metro Rail system, known as the Culver City Station. It is located at the northeast edge of downtown Culver City. Some easily accessible train stations in Culver City include Expo E Line, Robertson Transit Hub, Washington Blvd & National Blvd (Southbound).

Restaurants in Culver City

Café Vida

You should eat heartily, but also eat healthily. With the rising rate of health problems in the world today, Café Vida is prepared to offer its customers healthy food alternatives, with a menu containing low-fat Californian dishes such as salads and sandwiches that provide them with unique and delicious flavors.

Pasta Sisters

The Pasta Sisters is a casual and cozy Italian restaurant with expanded seating and two patios that serves fresh homemade pasta with delicious sauces. This is definitely a good place to keep in mind while looking rooms for rent in Culver City, California. The best part is, they have a food truck, too!

Metro Cafe

This restaurant in Culver City has been well-reviewed because of its tasty, sustainable, and healthy food options, good customer service, and affordable prices. It gives off a modern vibe and even provides outdoor seating. You can have breakfast here every day and enjoy lunch and dinner from Tuesday through to Sunday.

Parks in Culver City, California

  1. Culver City Park: Culver City Park is a gorgeous green space with a beautiful view. Spanning over 40 acres, this park provides amenities such as picnic tables, a dog park, a BBQ spot, a skate path, a playground for kids, and even a recreation hut.
  2. Kenneth Hahn State Recreation Area: Residents of Culver City have easy access to this park, which features large areas of native coastal sage scrub habitat, lawns, landscaped areas, picnic sites, tot lots, hiking trails, and more. It is regarded by locals as an outdoor wonderland and is great for a chill day out.
  3. Lindberg Park: This is a community park that offers 2 picnic areas with barbecues, a playground, athletic fields, and more. It’s a nice and quiet park where people of all ages can have a good time.

Recreational Activities in Culver City, California

Go on a Sony Pictures Studio Tour

You can’t possibly miss out on visiting the Sony Pictures Studio. If you want to glimpse a bit of Hollywood and feel like a star, then you should book a tour and enjoy a two-hour guided session that gives guests a look behind the screen. Here, you can visit the sets of famous shows and movies, like Wheel of Fortune, Jeopardy, and The Wizard of Oz.

Visit the Culver City Arts District

This district is located on Washington Boulevard between Helms and Fairfax Avenues. Here, you can explore and enjoy a unique collection of contemporary art galleries, artist studios, boutique restaurants, and specialty services.

The Culver City’s Farmers Market

This year-round, certified farmer’s market operates from 2pm to 7pm every Tuesday. It offers the surrounding community farm-fresh produce and locally crafted artisan foods. Here, everyone has access to fresh food ingredients, farm produce, and even seasonal craft activities.
