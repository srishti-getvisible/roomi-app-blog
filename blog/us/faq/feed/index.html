]]> If your listing is in ‘Insufficient’ or ‘Rejected’ status, you’ll receive an in-app message from our Listings Team letting you know what needs to be completed to get your room listing up and running.Common reasons for ‘Insufficient’ or ‘Rejected’ status:

  • We need to verify your residency at the address listed
  • The listing doesn’t have any interior photos or they are blurry
  • You don’t have a profile photo

As soon as we receive the requested information from you, we’ll have your listing up and running in no time! If you believe you did not receive an in-app message with this information, you may contact Roomi Support.

]]> Here are instructions for searching for a room on Roomi.

Web

  • Login to your account from our homepage by clicking Log In in the top right-hand corner
  • Click Browse Listings from the menu in the top left-hand corner
  • You can type a street, city, or area in the search bar to bring up any listings that are currently available. You can also set filters such as neighborhood, move date, neighborhood, duration, budget, and more. Click More Filters under the search bar to add any additional filters and then click Apply Filters

IOS

  • Tap Browse in the bottom left corner of the dashboard
  • Tap Listings in the top left cornerIf you want to search near your current location or an address, tap the map icon in the top left corner. You can also tap the filter icon in the top right corner
  • If you want to narrow your search even further. Tap Apply after selecting your filters to bring up your results.

Android

  • Tap the menu icon in the top left corner (represented by three stacked horizontal lines)
  • Tap Browse ListingsIf you want to search near your current location or an address, tap the map icon in the top right corner. You can also tap the filter icon in the bottom right corner
  • If you want to narrow your search even further. Once you have selected your filter options, tap the back arrow in the top left corner to bring up your results.
