30 Inspiring Quotes & Affirmations On Self Love

  • Posted byby Team Roomi
  • September 2, 2020

Self love is the act of genuinely loving who you are without reservation. When you achieve it, you feel loved unconditionally, and you don’t need the approval of others. More often said than done, self love is a term you’re bound to encounter on social media. Then why is it so hard to love ourselves? Sadly, we often treat ourselves in ways that we’d never treat others. The key is to essentially, remember that everything in your life flows from your relationship to yourself. Affirmations have a heart-warming way of healing us from within. All we have to do is allow ourselves that space to speak our affirmations.

But before we begin, let’s look at what affirmations are. Affirmations are positive statements that can help you to challenge and overcome self-sabotaging and negative thoughts. When you repeat them often and believe in them, you can start to make positive changes.

Learn to treat yourself like someone worthy of love, respect, and compassion, and your life will flow more effortlessly and joyfully than you can imagine.

Here are 30 quotes on self love, that you can use as affirmations every morning to lift your spirits, and give you a new perspective on things!

1. Give yourself the same care & attention that you give to others and watch yourself bloom.
2. Not perfect, but… hangin’ in there.
3. Embrace your own pace.
4. Nobody can steal your magic anymore.
5. You have enough strength to take on any challenge.
6. No matter what happens, you will be okay.
7. Stop waiting for permission to be who you want.
8. Dear Self, It’s alright if this year is still off to a weird start. There is still time. Love, me.
9. Self care is hard, but you’re worth it.
10. You are not alive just to pay bills and lose weight.
11. Searching for that one person to change your life? Look in the mirror.
12. As soon as I choose to see the light in the dark corners, I redirect my power towards what i want.
13. Stop letting the mirror control your confidence.
14. I believe I’m worthy of feeling good.
15. After everything, you are free to embrace this glorious day where life has risen from the shadows and love has found you anyway.
16. All along you were strong.
17. Wrapped in grace you will heal. You will carry the lessons you have gathered in this season into the next one.
18. Rest is just as important as the work.
19. Today I bless myself with peace and joy knowing that when i bless myself, my blessings carry out to others.

A small affirmation you can repeat to yourself at any time of the day.

20. I hope you understand you need your own love more than than they do.
21. Saying ‘no’ can be the best form of self-care.
22. Grateful for where I’m at, excited about where I’m going.
23. I deserve all that is good.
24. I am ready and open to receive miracles.
25. I am enough, I am whole, I am worthy.
26. Let me radiate only love, only light, only healing.
27. What I focus on is what I create. So I chose to focus on love and light.
28. A hard morning doesn’t mean a hard rest of the day.
29. I meet myself in stillness and we breathe.
30. The universe has big plans for me and it’s time to claim them. I am a super attractor!
