15 Best Things To Do In Colorado Springs

  • Posted byby Team Roomi
  • July 17, 2021

Colorado Springs is the most populated city in Colorado’s El Paso County. It’s a major tourist hub with plenty of places to visit and it’s also close to the base of Pikes Peak and the Garden of the Gods. This is also why the city, known affectionately as “Little London”, is a great place for people to unwind in nature, or even settle in this serene place. So, if you’re thinking of visiting or relocating to this stunning area, this guide is a must-read. Read on to discover the best things to do in Colorado Springs.

Exciting Outdoor Activities in Colorado Springs

1. Whitewater Rafting

This must-visit attraction covers all of the best things to do in Colorado Springs for adventurers! Thrilling all-day or half-day whitewater raft trips await you with reservations made through any of the river rafting companies in the region.

Website

2. Adventures Out West

Whether you’re looking for a high-thrill jeep ride or a high-flying balloon adventure over the beautiful landscape of the Pikes Peak region, any thrill-seeker will make lifelong memories here. You can also take a tour through the amazing Garden of the Gods on a Segway – surely one of the best things to do in Colorado Springs!

Website

3. Thrilling Jeep Tours

Take a ride along the rim of the Royal Gorge or tour the bright red monoliths of Red Canyon Park to explore some of the best places to visit in Colorado Springs. These Jeep Tours can also take you along the Gold Belt Tour through the late 1800’s mining districts of Cripple Creek and Victor.

Website

Must-Visit Museums in Colorado Springs

1. Penrose Heritage Museum

This museum is a tribute to the transportation history of The Springs, located just steps from the majestic Broadmoor Hotel. The Penrose Heritage Museum houses over 30 carriages, most with written histories of their usage displayed alongside them.

Website

2. Colorado Springs Pioneers Museum

Founded in 1979, the museum is a testament to the love for history that Colorado Springs still retains. The museum features an antique cage elevator built in 1917 that patrons can take a ride on to get a taste of the history of the building.

Plan your visit

3. ANA Money Museum

The Money Museum is home to America’s largest collections of coins, tokens, and paper money. Set against the beautiful, sprawling Colorado College campus, this has got to be one of the most unique places to visit in Colorado Springs!

Website

Sports to Try in Colorado Springs

1. Colorado Via Ferrata

Strap into the track cable for an exhilarating climbing activity through the spectacular mountain terrain. This activity is among the best things to do in Colorado Springs for adventure-lovers, with a two-hour guided tour under the close supervision of experienced guides.

Website

2. The Manitou Incline

This advanced-level trail is not for the faint of heart! With more than 2,000 feet in elevation gain in just under a mile, the Manitou Incline will push your endurance and lung capacity to their limits.

Events

3. Cloudscraper Zip Line

Undoubtedly, one of the best things to do in Colorado Springs is to soar more than 1,200 feet above the roaring Arkansas River at the Royal Gorge Bridge and Park. With 360-degree views of the Royal Gorge, you’ll have plenty of chances to take in the stunning views on this exhilarating ride!

Buy Tickets

Cultural Places To Visit in Colorado Springs

1. Opera Theatre of the Rockies

This is a great place to visit in Colorado Springs if you’re looking for world-class art exhibitions and theatre performances by professionals. Opera Theatre of the Rockies produces and presents exciting and memorable operas that spotlight talented local artists.

Calendar Events

2. Colorado Springs Fine Arts Center

Colorado Springs’ art and creative culture is thriving. Established in 1919, the city’s Fine Art Center provides innovative, educational, and multidisciplinary arts experiences that elevate the individual spirit and inspire community vitality.

Buy Tickets
Events

3. Rocky Mountain Women’s Film Institute

Rocky Mountain Women’s Film builds community around film by elevating the stories of women and others who are often unheard or unseen. As such, it is one of the best places to visit in Colorado Springs for an entertaining and meaningful cultural experience with friends.

Calendar Events

Explore Popular Bars, Shopping Centers & Restaurants in Colorado Springs

1. Jack Quinn’s Pub

One of the best things to do in Colorado Springs is to visit Jack QuinB’S Pub. It offers several beers on tap, but unlike most brewhouses, they mix a lot of their beers together. Try a Black and Tan, a Black and Brown, or a Black and Gold and you’ll reconsider how you’ve been enjoying beer.

Website

2. The Promenade Shops at Briargate

Alongside local shopping, The Promenade Shops at Briargate also offer dining options that are among some of the recommended places to visit in Colorado Springs. Whether you want to grab a bite at Ted’s Montana Grill, get coffee at Starbucks, tuck into a dessert at the Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory, or eat at Panera Bread there’s something for everyone to enjoy.

Website

3. Bird Dog BBQ

Bird Dog BBQ is a straight-up, large portion barbecue joint that will not fail to please. Choose from pulled pork, brisket, turkey, sausage (hotlink or Polish), ribs, or chicken, add a few classic sides, and you’ll be full for at least a day or two!
