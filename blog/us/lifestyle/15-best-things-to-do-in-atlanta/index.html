15 Best Things To Do In Atlanta

  • Posted byby Team Roomi
  • July 13, 2021

Atlanta, Georgia is a bustling metropolis known for its business-friendly environment and high quality of life. In addition to being a popular American city with ample entertainment options, there are also lots of great places to visit in Atlanta if you’re visiting from out of town or on vacation or even want to move here. So, to help you make the most of your time here and make a bunch of beautiful memories while you’re at it, here’s our list of the best things to do in Atlanta!

Must-Do Outdoor Activities in Atlanta

1. Hiking at Bartram Trail

One of the best places to visit in Atlanta is the Bartram Trail, which is an easy 2.7-mile hike through woodland with plenty of scenic views along the way. The trail runs alongside a stream that makes it even more picturesque and tranquil!

  • Cost: Free
Website
Directions

2. Enjoy a walk at the Atlanta Botanical Garden

In the Botanical Garden, you’re guaranteed to see at least one type of plant or animal that you have never seen before. There’s also a range of events, exhibitions, and classes you can get involved with. So, if you’re looking to take some time out for yourself and get closer to nature, this is surely one of the best things to do in Atlanta for you.

  • Cost: $22.95
  • Timings: 9 am – 5 pm
Book Now
Directions
Website

3. Visit the Stone Mountain Park

Stone Mountain is known for its natural beauty and historical importance. There are nine different trails up the mountain, including one that winds through a nature reserve and leads to a gorgeous waterfall and an aerial tramway. Hitch a ride for an experience that will blow your mind!

  • Cost: $32- $35
  • Timings: 5 am-12 am
Website
Directions

Sports in Atlanta

1. Get a chance to catch Atlanta Hawks live

This professional basketball team plays in the Southeast Division of the Eastern Conference in the NBA. Catching the Atlanta Hawks playing live at their home match at State Farm Arena can be a real treat and is one of the best things to do in Atlanta for sports lovers!

  • Cost: The average price to attend a game during the season is $51
Tickets
Website

2. Visit the Mercedes-Benz Stadium

The Mercedes-Benz Stadium is one of the most technologically advanced stadiums in the world. A visit to the stadium is one of the best things to do in Atlanta, especially on game days when the crowds fill the arena to cheer on their favorite teams. Or, if you’d prefer a quieter visit, you can also explore the stadium on off days.

  • Cost: The average cost of a ticket for an event is $115
Website
Directions

3. Watch Atlanta Braves playing live

A quintessential American game, it’s great to catch Atlanta’s baseball team playing a home match live at Truist Park. Going to the match is one of the best things to do in the city because you get to see the players up close and experience all the thrills and excitement firsthand!

  • Cost: The average cost of a ticket for a game is $67

Animal attractions & museums in Atlanta

1. Visit Zoo Atlanta

As the most famous zoo in Georgia, a visit to Zoo Atlanta is a great way to spend an afternoon. Visitors can learn about various animal habitats while having fun at one of the many special events hosted by the zoo, like wine tastings and cooking classes. If you’re looking for fun places to visit in Atlanta for all the family, try the zoo for sure!

  • Cost: $26.99
  • Timings: 9 am – 4 pm
Book Now
Website
Directions

2. Explore ocean life at Georgia Aquarium

Georgia Aquarium is one of the largest aquariums in the world where visitors can see whale sharks, beluga whales, penguins, and more. They can also watch and learn at animal shows including dolphin performances and sea lion feedings – definitely one of the best things to do in Atlanta for animal lovers!

  • Cost: $36.95
  • Timings: 9 am – 9 pm
Tickets

Directions

3. Visit the High Museum of Art

The High Museum of Art is one of the greatest places to visit in Atlanta if you’re looking for something different from the regular touristy destinations in the city. It provides visitors with hundreds of different art forms from all around the world and always has new exhibitions and events going on!

  • Cost: $14.50
  • Timings: 12 pm – 5 pm
Website
Directions

Culture in Atlanta

1. Catch a show at Alliance Theatre

Nestled in the city’s Sweet Auburn historic district, The Alliance Theatre is dedicated to providing high-quality entertainment while using art as a catalyst for positive community change. The theatre reflects the cultural diversity of the region and is one of the best places to visit in the city!

  • Cost: $35 per adult; $15 per child
  • Timings: Various

2. Enjoy a light-hearted evening at Atlanta Magic Theater

The magic theater is an intimate live comedy experience with your favorite local comedians and the best touring acts from around the country. Laugh all night long, drink some drinks, and get to know another side of the city in a fun environment that mixes improv comedy with stand-up comedy!

  • Cost: $10.00-$25.00
  • Timings: Friday 6:30 pm –10 pm; Saturday 4:30–11 pm

Directions

3. Listen in to the Atlanta Symphony Orchestra

Attending the AOS is a great way to spend the evening. As many already know, classical music can be enjoyed by all ages and backgrounds, and here at AOS, a variety of high-profile events are hosted throughout the year!

  • Cost: $64
  • Timings: Various

Explore restaurants, bars & shopping centers in Atlanta

1. Savor meals at Colonnade

The Colonnade is a favorite restaurant with locals in Atlanta. A classic American diner with an eclectic, retro vibe, the restaurant provides a wide variety of delicious dishes for its customers. Plus, their famous fried chicken has been drawing in visitors for decades!

  • Cost: $11-$30
  • Timings: 5 pm – 9 pm
Website
Directions

2. Enjoy a great meal at Mary Mac’s Tearoom

One of the best-known restaurants in Atlanta is Mary Mac’s Tearoom. Despite its quaint and unassuming name, this iconic eatery is famous for its sassy servers, kitschy atmosphere, and delicious Southern-style cooking.

  • Cost: $11-$30
  • Timings: 11 am – 9 pm
Website
Directions

3. Make a day out of shopping at Lenox Square

Lenox Square is a shopaholic’s paradise! It’s an upscale, enclosed luxury shopping mall located in the Buckhead district. There are 225 retail shops and restaurants spread across two levels in the mall, making it one of the best places to visit in Atlanta for all of your shopping needs!

  • Cost: Entry is free
  • Timings: 10 am – 7 pm
Website
