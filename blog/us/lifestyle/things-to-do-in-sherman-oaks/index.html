15 Best Things To Do In Sherman Oaks

  • Posted byby Team Roomi
  • September 1, 2021

With a plethora of eateries, parks, and weekly farmer’s markets, Sherman Oaks is bustling with activity. And if you’ve just moved to the area and are looking for things to do and new places to eat in the city, we’ve got you covered. Read on to find out more about the best things to do in Sherman Oaks!

Outdoors in Sherman Oaks

1. Explore the wonderful Sepulveda Basin Wildlife Reserve

Sprawled across 230 acres, this wildlife reserve features well-marked walking trails, perfect for hiking or strolling and spotting some of the animals that call the reserve home. There’s a picturesque lake here, too, where you can catch a glimpse of some butterflies, birds, or lizards. One of the more famous Sherman Oaks attractions, this wildlife refuge is a must-visit for those who want to explore the wilderness.

Learn More

2. Find your Zen energy at the Japanese Garden

Located near Van Nuys, the Japanese Garden covers seven acres of land, and it’s the perfect place to take a relaxing stroll. It was designed to resemble the flourishing 18th and 19th-century gardens in Japan. For those who love peace and quiet and feeling closer to nature, the Japanese Garden is the perfect weekend destination.

Sports in Sherman Oaks

1. Stop by Van Nuys Sherman Oaks Recreation Center

The Van Nuys Sherman Oaks Recreation Center features a park, pathways, a pool, plus a few athletic fields. One of the best things to do in Sherman Oaks on a sunny day is to visit this recreation center for a lap around the pool or a game of frisbee. Arrange a day out with your friends or family and make the most of your day breathing in the fresh air at the Van Nuys Sherman Oaks Park.

Learn More

2. Enjoy a game of laser tag at Ultrazone!

Tired of frequenting the same old haunts with your friends? Playing a game of laser tag at Ultrazone is one of the best things to do in Sherman Oaks when you’re looking to take a break from your typical routine. Catch up with your friends in a whole new way with this ‘Ultimate Laser Tag Adventure’.

Costs: $13 per person for a single round; $12 per person for members; $11 per person when you buy 12 games or more.

Learn More

3. Enjoy a round of Miniature Golf at the Sherman Oaks Castle Park

Miniature golf may not be the most athletic activity out there, but it sure is a lot of fun. Apart from rides and other attractions, Sherman Oaks Castle Park features an 18-hole miniature golf course, which makes for a fun outing. Challenge your friends to a hole-in-one, or just enjoy the madness of trying to get through the adventurous course.

Learn More

Museums in Sherman Oaks

1. Enjoy the picturesque view at The Getty

Whether you’re interested in the scenic beauty, the interesting architecture, or its collection of fine photography, The Getty is one of the top Sherman Oaks attractions. You’ll be able to grab food and drinks, and shop for books, toys, and more while you’re here.

Learn More

Culture in Sherman Oaks

1. Get your fill of fresh local produce at Sherman Oaks Tuesday Market

Apart from fresh produce, you can also find sweet treats, eggs, and meat at the Tuesday Market. This farmer’s market is held once a week throughout the year, so you have plenty of opportunities to visit it.

Learn More

2. Walk down the Ventura Boulevard

One of the oldest roads in San Fernando Valley, you may be familiar with the name Ventura Boulevard if you’re a fan of Tom Petty (or John Mayer!). Although it’s 18 miles long, you don’t need to walk all of it to find out what it has to offer. Plus, cycling along Ventura Boulevard is surely one of the best things to do in Sherman Oaks.

Learn More

3. Stop by the Skirball Cultural Center

One of the city’s hidden gems, you need to stop by this cultural center if you’ve got the time. Skirball is a Jewish cultural institution that is part museum, and part performance centre, and educational facility. No matter what your cultural or religious background is, you’ll always be welcome here.

Learn More

4. Explore the large selection of CDs & Vinyl’s at Freakbeat Records

Featuring everything from rock to jazz and much more besides, the collection of new and used CDs and vinyl at Freakbeat Records contains something for everyone. Here, you’ll also find books, magazines, and vintage posters of your favorite bands and artists. If old school’s your style, then Freakbeat Records is the place to be

Learn More

Restaurants in Sherman Oaks

1. Satisfy your taste buds at the Local Peasant

From local craft beers to classic burgers, sandwiches, and pizzas, you can enjoy your favorite American comfort foods at the Local Peasant. The dishes on the menu feature locally sourced ingredients which add to the delicious flavor. If you’re looking for restaurants in Sherman Oaks for a delightful meal and cozy ambiance, you’ve found the spot!

Learn More

2. Try out the Mexican food at Casa Vega

Ask any local and they will tell you that Casa Vega serves the best Mexican food in Sherman Oaks. Located along the Ventura Boulevard, it’s not unusual to run into celebs here, either. From margaritas to quesadillas, you won’t be disappointed with what you order.

Learn More

3. Explore the unique concept of The Joint Eatery

Combining a coffee shop and a fish market, who would have thought that such a concept could work? Somehow, the Joint Eatery manages to pull it off, and the ambiance is second to none. The coffee and fish areas are separated by a wall thanks to its large space. When you get hungry in the middle of answering work emails, you can grab some of the freshest seafood dishes in Sherman Oaks just next door.

Learn More

Shopping Centres in Sherman Oaks

1. Get your fashion fill at the Westfield Fashion Square Mall

From Aldo to Macy’s, you’ll find it all at the Westfield Fashion Square Mall. When you want to keep up with the latest fashion trends, head to this mall and you won’t be disappointed. Not just for shopping, the Westfield Fashion Square Mall is also home several of the top restaurants in Sherman Oaks.

Learn More

2. Stop by the Sherman Oaks Galleria

Catch a movie, enjoy a relaxing spa day, or get your fill of shopping at the Sherman Oaks Galleria. With numerous restaurants and notable stores, time will fly by as you explore everything there is to do at this galleria.
