18 Best Things To Do In Corpus Christi

  • Posted byby Team Roomi
  • July 5, 2021

Corpus Christi is a port city on the Gulf of Mexico in the state of Texas. It’s one of the greatest places in North America for kiteboarding, windsurfing, kite flying, and sailing due to strong wind speeds. The city has even hosted the World Championships in Windsurfing. These reasons also make it a great place to live in and experience the unique lifestyle the metropolis offers. Read on to discover the best things to do in Corpus Christi!

Exciting Outdoor Activities in Corpus Christi

1. Padre Island National Seashore

This spectacular 70-mile length of white sand beach, picturesque dunes, and warm ocean water is just a 15-minute drive away from downtown Corpus Christi. This beach is home to some of the best things to do in Corpus Christi, including swimming, fishing, beachcombing, windsurfing, camping, and bird watching.

Plan Your Visit

2. Malaquite Beach

There are numerous serene beaches near the city with Instagram-worthy views. This beach on Padre Island is among the best places to visit in Corpus Christi for camping!

Website

3. North Beach

Visitors refer to this beach as a little piece of heaven on the bay. The waves are generally modest, making it one of the best places to visit in Corpus Christi with kids – or on your own! There are trash cans dotted across the beach, and the beach is properly maintained.

Website

Don’t Miss Out On The Sports Scene in Corpus Christi

1. Catch a Corpus Christi Hooks Baseball Game

This Double-A Affiliate of the Houston Astros is Corpus Christi’s Minor League Baseball team, but there’s more than just baseball at Whataburger Field. Friday night games mean Friday fireworks! It’s one of the exciting places to visit in the city, since watching the show’s a guaranteed blast.

Website

2. Coastal Bend Kayaking

For a water adventure with a little less intensity, kayaking is a great option. Located in the gorgeous Redfish Bay, it’s one of the best things to do in Corpus Christi for people of all ages!

Tours

3. Floating Yoga at Water Dog

Ever tried going into downward dog on a paddleboard? In the middle of a bay? Here’s your chance! Located on a houseboat in the downtown area, it’s one of the recommended places to visit in Corpus Christi for a wholesome experience!

Website

Must Visit Museums in Corpus Christi

1. Texas Surf Museum

The Texas Surf Museum, the state’s only surf museum, is close by, allowing enthusiasts to learn more about the sport’s distinctive history and culture in the Lone Star State. Even better, the museum is free, so it’s among the best places to visit in Corpus Christi for an inexpensive outing!

  • Timings: 11am- 7pm
Website

2. Corpus Christi Museum of Science & History

At this museum, get ready to immerse yourself in hands-on activities and discovery! The 40,000 square feet of display space at the museum is made up of carefully chosen exhibits showcasing the region’s culture.

Admission Prices

Buy Tickets
Website

3. Selena Museum

Selena Quintanilla is known as the Queen of Tejano Music, and she is a beloved figure in Corpus Christi. Her music brought the Latinx community together, and her enthusiasm inspired people all over the world.

  • Timings: Monday – Friday, 10am – 4pm

Cultural Places To Visit in Corpus Christi

1. Texas State Aquarium

Once you reach the city, one of the best things to do in Corpus Christi is to visit the Texas State Aquarium. It offers insightful information and entertaining displays on the Gulf of Mexico’s diverse aquatic life.

Buy Tickets
Website

2. Centennial House

Centennial House is the city’s oldest structure, dating back to 1849. It was also utilized as a Civil War hospital at one point. This lovely old house has been entirely renovated and is now accessible to the public for tours. It’s decorated with period antiques and artifacts.

Know More

3. Old Town Square

At Old Town Square, become a part of the city’s history as you enjoy one of the best things to do in Corpus Christi. Connect with historical reenactors depicting the families who previously resided in the Heritage Park houses and capture a memory with an antique picture booth. Shop from local and regional craft merchants beneath a maypole, snuggle up with some fuzzy friends in the petting zoo, and relax while listening to live music and eating delicious food from local restaurants in Corpus Christi.

Know More

Explore Popular Restaurants in Corpus Christi

1. Thai Spice

When people talk about Corpus Christi, they’re more likely to talk about the weather, culture, or beaches than they are about the food. However, when it comes to a great dinner and Instagram-worthy delicacies, the Thai Spice does not disappoint.

Website

2. The Yardarm

You’re in for a treat if you haven’t yet tried the city’s delicious seafood dishes. Explore this local favorite, a tried-and-true establishment that has been winning over hearts (and stomachs) for years.

Website

3. Kiko’s Mexican Food Restaurant and Cantina

Kiko’s is a family-owned restaurant that serves flavorful Mexican dishes using family recipes made from scratch. They offer appetizers, soup, salad, lunch and dinner plates, and much more. Once you’ve enjoyed a cocktail or two from their Papalote bar, you’ll definitely want to visit again!

Website

Bars & Shopping Centers in Corpus Christi

1. Rockit’s Whiskey Bar & Saloon

The fun doesn’t end in the city with sundown; you will find plenty of places to have fun with friends after-hours. From live music to drinks at Rockit’s, you’re in for a night out that you won’t forget!

Website
Drink Menu

2. La Palmera

Start your shopping spree with an adventure to La Palmera. With over 100 shops and restaurants in Corpus Christi’s La Palmera, it’s a premiere shopping and dining destination in the Coastal Bend.

Website

3. Bleu Frog Mercantile

Feel like exploring more? Go to Bleu Frog Mercantile for fine art, antiques, gourmet gifts, and Texan and coastal home goods.
