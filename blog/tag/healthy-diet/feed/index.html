]]> If you’re struggling to come up with meal ideas that fit your busy schedule, we hear you! Juggling life, work and the kitchen all at once is tough! Here is a list of prepared meal ideas that’ll help you cook easy healthy meals within a few minutes. Roomi has put together tasty recipes along with low carb ideas which are super healthy in nature.

Even if you’re not the most innovative person in the kitchen and only have limited ingredients at hand, these easy and healthy meals will guide you. Start your stress-free journey now and cook meals that require less than 20 minutes of your time.

Effortless prepared meal ideas to try now!

https://in.pinterest.com/pin/19492210902018565/

Paleo Shepherd’s Pie

Don’t let the cauliflower-mash topping make you skeptical. This dish is one of the most nutrient-rich prepared meal ideas that’ll satisfy your taste buds right away! This is a healthier take on a classic pie but remains just as comforting with hearty ground beef and tender veggies, flavored with onion, cumin, coriander, and red pepper. Sounds so yum!

Make your prepared meal ideas extra special with Asian Glazed Meatballs

https://in.pinterest.com/pin/238198267783828385/

This dish is also low-carb and gluten free. Feel free to make a big batch of these meatballs on Sunday and stash them in the freezer. This way, you can cook them off throughout the week without having to worry that they’ll go bad. Asian Glazed Meatballs With Zucchini Noodles Meal Prep are truly a treasure when it comes to prepared meal ideas to try!

Instant-Pot Chicken Cacciatore With Zucchini Noodles

When you try different cuisines such as spaghetti, you are drifted away to the Italian countryside! Pasta is a versatile and inexpensive staple but very high in carbs. Thus, with zucchini noodles you get to taste the real essence of Italian chicken. In addition, it’s one of the most easy and healthy meals to make with mushrooms, tomatoes, and herbs.

Satay Beef: One of the most efficient prepared meal ideas

https://in.pinterest.com/pin/54043264269589203/

Some say prepared meal ideas are wholesome when there’s beef, and they’re absolutely right. Satay Beef prep recipe is not short on flavor! This recipe is a cinch to throw together, and totally make-ahead friendly. However, keep in mind that the beef needs to marinate for at least 30 minutes, so plan accordingly. Serve it up with cauliflower rice and green beans for a delicious low carb lunch option.

Instant-Pot Chinese Sesame Chicken

Eat this versatile chicken with a side of veggies for dinner, and simply reheat the next day for a salad or lettuce wrap. Yes, it’s that simple! Basically this recipe is not deep fried, so you have here one of the most easy, healthy, low carb meals. Yet, it still offers bold spicy-sweet flavors, and a deep nutty sesame note. Who wouldn’t want to try this!

Healthy meals made easy with Caprese avocado salad

https://in.pinterest.com/pin/1688918599491842/

This light, refreshing prepared meal is loaded with fresh mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, basil and avocado with a sweet balsamic reduction. The prepping time is less and the salad will leave you full and satisfied. This vibrant salad also serves perfect as an appetizer or entrée when you are hosting a get together!

Chicken & Sugar Snap Pea Stir-Fry

Plump, shiny sugar snap peas are perfect for stir-frying, because they cook quickly and still maintain a slight crunch. In this recipe, they’re paired with thinly sliced chicken and Asian flavors for a delicious weeknight meal that comes together in minutes. And will be over within seconds!

These prepared meal ideas will take you, from feeling constantly stressed out about cooking to being cool, collected and in control. Try these easy and healthy meals which will soon be your go-to comfort food within no time!
