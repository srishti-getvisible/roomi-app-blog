What is professional growth?

Professional growth or professional development essentially refers to learning new skills that make you better in your career. It keeps you from going obsolete in an ever-evolving job market. A professional development plan lets you outline the skills you need to excel in your career, stay up-to-date with trends, and carve an individualistic presence for yourself in your industry.
