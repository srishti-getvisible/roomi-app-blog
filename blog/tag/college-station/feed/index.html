How transit-friendly is College Station

1. Walkability

If you’re fond of walking and hiking, you’re going to love College Station! The city has many wonderful and scenic hiking trails located all around the city. Give it a shot on a cool weekend when you feel like being around nature. Just remember to bring loads of water!

2. Taxis

There are many taxi services around College Station that are extremely convenient and easy to access. Yes, they’re one of the more expensive modes of transportation. But, hey, sometimes you just gotta hail a taxi and be on your way!

3. Public Transportation

The Texas A&M Bus System is going to be your life-saver when you’re here, trust us! It’s the easiest, not to mention the cheapest, way to get around the city. You can access the bus schedule to keep track of the timings. Whether you want to travel within the campus or go out, these buses will take you anywhere!

4. Airports

College Station has its very own regional airport called the Easterwood Airport. The airport operates daily flights to Dallas/Fort Worth, Houston, and seasonal service to Birmingham.

5. Cycling

Biking is an extremely popular mode of transportation in College Station, especially amongst the students. Texas A&M University is very bike-friendly and has numerous bike racks around campus. So, if you’re going to be a student here, biking is a great and cheap way to get around!
