3. Taxis in Louisville

Louisville offers great taxi services, with zTrip at the airport. Life in Louisville isn’t complete without alternative ride sharing companies, such as Uber and Lyft.

4. Public transportation in Louisville

The Transit Authority of River City (TARC) is the major public transportation provider for the Louisville, Kentucky, United States metro area, which includes parts of Southern Indiana. Keep in mind, single ride – $2.75 exact change. Also, cash fare does not include transfer trips.

5. Airports in Louisville

Being the 16th largest city in the United States, Louisville has 3 main airports. These airports and their websites offer facilities that cater to flyers’ comfort.

  • Louisville International Airport
