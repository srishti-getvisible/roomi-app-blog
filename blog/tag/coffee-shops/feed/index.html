]]> Aside from Culver City’s rich movie-making history, the city is known for showcasing the evolution of the modern-day coffee shop. From the Conservatory’s vintage-style café to The Boy & The Bear’s serene coworking space and the modern and minimalist Cognoscenti Coffee Bar — your choices for a good cup of joe are limitless in this city. And don’t forget the delightful meals and baked goods you can pair them with! If you’re planning to visit the area, check out these 10 best coffee shops in Culver City.

1. Bar Nine

Known for its exclusive menu of espresso, espresso and milk, pour-over, and cold brew, Bar Nine is one of the most straightforward coffee shops in Culver City. Their beans are sourced and roasted to serve their customers the best cup of coffee at any time of the day. They also offer an all-day brunch option for a great dining experience in the city.

2. The Conservatory for Coffee, Tea & Cocoa

The Conservatory is a café that knows its coffee well, and when you’re living in LA, good coffee is a must! The Conservatory has an extensive menu of coffee, tea, and cocoa, and if you’re having a hard time choosing a drink, their friendly staff are happy to help.

3. Metro Café

For a quick bite or coffee run, Metro Café is one of the best cafés in LA! This coffee shop in Culver City is attached to a Travelodge and opens up at breakfast to serve meals and great coffee. It’s also pet-friendly, so you can dine alfresco with your furry friend.

Menu

4. The Boy & The Bear

If you’re looking for coworking cafés in the city, The Boy & The Bear offers a serene place that serves exquisite Colombian-style coffee. You can shop for beans specially roasted to the taste of Colombia, and they even have apparel and tumblers that will surely catch your eye. Check out their website to find out more!

5. Cognoscenti Coffee Bar

If you’re looking for a trendy, minimalist coffee shop in Culver City, Cognoscenti is the place for an afternoon pick-me-up. They have beans from roasters worldwide — and one sip of their coffee will have you coming back for more!

6. Café 70 Degrees

If you’re living in LA and craving desserts, Café 70 Degrees is your home for sweet treats! They serve a variety of baked goods and hand-crafted organic coffee in a cozy environment. This cafe is an excellent place for families or other groups to meet up and enjoy delicious cakes and treats together.

Menu

7. Blue Bottle Coffee

Blue Bottle Coffee is the place for coffee connoisseurs! This coffee shop in Culver City used to be an auto body shop but is now a prestigious café with its own custom drip bar. Enjoy an assortment of pastries, coffee, and merchandise when you visit this trendy cafe chain.

View Website

8. Super Domestic Coffee

A coffee shop and a cyclery, Super Domestic Coffee is a place you’ll definitely remember. It’s a neighborhood cafe with several types of coffee (their signature espressos are a must-try!) and various baked treats that are too delicious to share!

View Website
Menu

9. Spot Café & Lounge

There are many cafés in LA, but Spot Café & Lounge is one you should definitely check out! The coffee shop is near a residential area, so it’s always packed with friendly locals. Spot Café has daily specials for coffee and a wide variety of food and beverages you’ll love to try.

Visit Website

10. Kings Café

Kings Café is a long-time coffee shop in Culver City. They offer affordable and delectable American cuisine dishes and wonderful coffee with impeccable service. This classic cafe is a must-visit when you’re in Culver City!
