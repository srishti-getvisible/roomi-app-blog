How transit friendly is Dublin?

1. Trams

The tram system in Dublin, which is also known as the Luas, is an efficient way to get around the city. Riding the Luas is convenient and comfortable as well as fun. There are stops all over Dublin, so you can get from one place to another quite easily. Trams are a great way of getting around because they’re quick and easy and good for the environment. You should try the Luas when looking for rooms for rent in Dublin!

2. Buses

The Dublin Bus network is the primary provider of public transport in the city of Dublin. There are over 100 routes which cover the whole of the city. If you want to look for rooms for rent in Dublin, then you can try getting around the city on a bus. This way, you will already be familiar with the bus system when you move!

3. Taxi

Taxis are the most reliable method of transport to get you to your destination at night or at times when you are in a hurry but can’t find a bus or a tram. Even though taxis aren’t the cheapest way to travel in Dublin, they are still a great way to get to places without having to get behind the wheel yourself!

4. Walkability

In a city where it seems half of the population commutes by foot, you might find yourself walking more than your fair share. It can also be an opportunity to make sure you’re maximizing your time spent on two feet.
