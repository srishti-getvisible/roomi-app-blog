]]> Stepping out of your comfort zone and traveling to a new city is super rewarding. We get to experience new cultures and beautiful locations, and meet new people along the way. Some people believe you can’t truly experience a city unless you’ve lived there a while, so they head online to find their next temp home! Airbnb is one of the most popular booking sites for finding accommodation in cities all over the world. That said, it’s definitely not the only website you can use. With its surge in popularity, Airbnb listings often sell out fast! And, with such high demand, the prices have also shot up. So if you’re searching for an Airbnb alternative, we’ve got a list to start you off. Read on to discover websites like Airbnb that can make your travel hassle-free.

Top Sites Like Airbnb

Looking for an apartment to rent with some cool people? Or just looking for somewhere short-term to stay while you stopover before your next city? This list of sites like Airbnb has something for everyone – dive in!

1. Roomi

Roomi is one of the best room finders out there for many reasons. Let’s explore them below!

No-fee listings

No-fee listings mean that, whether you book a room or a whole place, you’ll be paying no extra charges on top of the rent. It’s a cheaper alternative to Airbnb because what you see is what you pay.

Pet-friendly apartments

If you’re traveling or moving with your pet, Roomi’s got you covered with its pet-friendly filters. Whether you have a dog, cat, or something entirely different, you can find listings that welcome your little friend with open arms.

Subletting

Roomi offers a subletting service to take the complications out of subletting. That means you can rent out your place to other tenants, to save some money and do some more traveling! With secure in-app messaging and a robust verification process, you can sublet your apartment anywhere in the world that allows it!

Amenities

Whether you’re leasing a place for a short period or a longer duration, comfort is key. If it’s a doorman you need, a gym to unwind in, or an in-house washer to do laundry, you can choose from over 30 amenities on Roomi.

Listing Type and House Rules

If all of this sounds too good to be true, it’s not! There are even more benefits of using Roomi as an Airbnb alternative. For example, you can filter by entire place, own room, or even a shared room. So both groups and solo travelers have enough options to choose from.

Once you’ve filtered by listing, you can also select house rules like whether smoking is allowed or guests can come over.

To sum it up, if you want a one-stop solution for all of your accommodation needs, Roomi is the way to go. They offer both a mobile app and website so all you have to do is visit this link and take it from there.

2. VRBO

Another Airbnb alternative, VRBO is an abbreviation for Vacation Rental By Owner. As the name indicates, it’s great for renting places if you’re on vacation. It’s got over a million properties worldwide in the most popular travel destinations. However, if you’re looking to relocate, you might find this an expensive long-term rental. You also have to pay a booking fee when you select your rental.

3. Booking.com

Another website like Airbnb, Booking.com lets you filter through rooms and apartments. With an easy-to-use interface, this site can help you find a rental anywhere in the world. And what’s more? It’s usually a much cheaper alternative to Airbnb. However, you’re unlikely to be able to split the cost of renting with a roommate unless you’re traveling with them.

4. TurnKey

This Airbnb alternative offers something completely different. Instead of a key to your rental, you get a passcode to your door – completely eliminating the need for a middleman. It caters exclusively to vacationers and holiday goers. Unfortunately, it currently only caters to the United States so it’s not the best fit if you’re planning on going international.

5. Agoda Homes

Definitely a cheaper alternative to Airbnb, Agoda Homes guarantees one of the best prices on the internet. In fact, if you can find a cheaper price for the same property, they’re willing to refund the difference. It offers both hotels and apartments for rent with a host of amenities to choose from.

6. Home Exchange

This Airbnb alternative offers a fun way to travel. As the name suggests, you can swap houses with someone in another part of the world for your vacation. However, you will need to pay to sign up. And more importantly, while this could be great for a short period of time, you can’t relocate permanently using this website. If you’re a little iffy with the idea of letting someone else take over your property, you can choose to host people instead of swapping homes. This lets you earn ‘balloons’ that you can later use to travel.

7. TripAdvisor Rentals

Another popular service that’s similar to Airbnb, TripAdvisor Rentals offers vacation homes with the amenities you can find at home. One of the biggest advantages of using this site is that you get access to reviews of past renters. This will help you get an idea of exactly what you can expect from your rental. However, while it’s similar to Airbnb in terms of vacation homes, it doesn’t offer any long-term rentals such as apartments.

Which is the best Airbnb alternative?

If you’re looking for the best Airbnb alternative, try different options from this list until you find the right fit. But to start off with, we recommend choosing Roomi. With cheaper prices, many amenities to choose from, and filters that’ll help you find that perfect place, it’s got it all! In addition to all of this, you’ve also got the certainty of absolute security because of their thorough screening process and 24-hour content and moderation team. Visit the website and start planning today!
