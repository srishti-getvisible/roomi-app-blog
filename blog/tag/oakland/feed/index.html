How transit friendly is Oakland

1. Airports in Oakland

Oakland has access to three major airports. These are Oakland International Airport, San Francisco International Airport, and San Jose International Airport. The nearest of these is the Oakland International airport that lies just 4 mi South of Downtown. You can easily find taxis or buses from the airport to the main city. Air connectivity is great, if you are looking for rooms for rent in Oakland.

2. Taxis in Oakland

You can easily use taxis in Oakland and they are generally safe and convenient. However, it is advisable to book one in advance and you will need to call for pickup in most areas. Taxi rates in Oakland are generally higher than most cities. Ride-sharing apps like Uber and Lyft are also available. They can be great if you are looking for rooms for rent in Oakland in an out-of-the-way area.

3. Subway in Oakland

Oakland, along with the rest of the San Francisco Bay area is served by the Bay Area Rapid Transit. (BART) This subway system serves San Jose, San Francisco, Oakland, and nearby counties. If you are looking for rooms for rent in Oakland, be sure to check for availability. You can find the schedules and maps for the BART routes on their website.

4. Buses in Oakland

Buses in Oakland are operated by AC Transit. It is an Oakland-based public transit agency that serves the Oakland city and parts of the San Francisco Bay Area. AC Transit also operates ‘Transbay’ routes to San Francisco and areas in San Mateo and Santa Clara counties. A local day pass costs $5 and allows unlimited transfers. Be sure to check availability if you are finding rooms for rent in Oakland.

Cultural Activites in Oakland

  • Fox Theater: This theater was built in 1928 but It closed its doors in 1970. It stood empty until 2009 when it was reopened as a music venue that is a hub of culture and entertainment. A great place to visit if you are looking for rooms for rent in Oakland and enjoy music!
  • Oaktoberfest: Oakland’s answer to the Oktoberfest, this event is held on the last weekend of September. Try a variety of brews, or enjoy non-alcoholic options as well.
  • Chabot Space and Science Center: Head here to explore a variety of exhibits or megadome movies on space. You can find many hands-on opportunities to learn about space and use the telescopes. A fun place if you are looking for rooms for rent in Oakland and enjoy learning about science and space.

Dining in Oakland

  • Golden Lotus Vegan Restaurant: A Vietnamese restaurant that offers unique dishes and cuisines and dishes at a budget rate. Described as ‘Asian-Fusion Vegan’, all dishes are made with organic ingredients. A great stop if you are looking for rooms for rent in Oakland and are vegan!
  • Guadalajara: This Mexican restaurant and bar serves from a gigantic menu ranging from simple salads to seafood. A local favorite, it is a great place to grab some delicious Mexican food with a drink. Vegetarian options are available too and there is accessible on-site parking.
  • Arizmendi: Arizmendi is a local bakery that makes artisan bread, morning pastries, and gourmet pizza. A favorite among locals, it serves fresh delights every day and is a great place to pick up a snack if you are in the area.
