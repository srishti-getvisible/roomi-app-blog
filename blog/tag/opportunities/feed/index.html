]]> Looking to move to a vibrant new city? Boston could be the perfect place for you! Now, there are a lot of advantages of living in Boston. Located in New England on the coast of Massachusetts, the city is home to rich culture and opportunities. But that’s just one of the many reasons to love to Boston. Offering the best of city and small-town life, living in Boston is a great alternative to living in New York City as it provides you with a city environment that isn’t as bustling and urban. So, the question isn’t “is moving to Boston a good idea?”, the question is “should I move to Boston?”.

As the perfect happy medium, it offers many advantages of city life, such as walkability and countless shops and restaurants, as well as the advantages of suburbia, including lots of greenery.

Whether you’ve recently graduated from college or you and a roommate are looking for a fresh new city, Boston is the perfect place to live during your 20’s.

So, without any further ado, here are 5 reasons why Boston should be the next city that you move to!

1. Advantages of living in Boston: the walkability

One of the perks of living in Boston is that it’s easy to get around the city on foot. From visiting the coffee shop around the corner to walking to work, Boston is a very easy city to navigate. Not to mention the the picturesque beauty of Boston makes walking to work an outstanding experience! So, the next time you ask yourself if moving to Boston is a good idea, just look at these beautiful cobblestone streets!

Fun fact: Boston residents have a historic love for walking. The Freedom Trail located in the city was one of the first historic walking tours in the country. It’s just one of the many reasons to love to Boston!

2. There’s always something to do

You’ll never get bored in Boston! So, if you’re looking for a fun and vibrant city, Boston is the place for you. From sporting events (go Red Sox!) and festivals to museums, parks, concerts, and restaurants, there’s always something to keep you (and your roomi!) busy in this city.

For example, if you’re looking for a fun place to go on the weekends, Faneuil Hall is an awesome place to shop and eat endless lobster rolls! Not to mention, Downtown Boston has a number of shops and restaurant options to keep any shopaholic or foodie content.

3. Advantages of living in Boston: Job opportunities

Boston is a city of opportunity as it is home to a variety of industries. Because it’s home to some of the biggest companies located, including Amazon, Google, and Microsoft, it’s is a great place for those looking for jobs in the finance, technology, and science fields. But, if you’re planning on making the move, be sure to start your job search early!

4. There’s nature!

If you want the convenience of a city but also prioritize being in nature, Boston is the coastal city for you as it gives you the best of both worlds! Because one of the great advantages of living in Boston is that you get to experience New England’s breathtaking weather. Plus, what they say is true, there’s nothing like fall in New England! The city is home to numerous parks, including the Boston Common and Public Garden. Boston Common is equipped with picnic areas, a carousel, and The Frog Pond. But most importantly, you can ride the infamous swan boats- one of the main reasons to love to Boston!

5. There’s an emphasis on health and wellbeing

As a young person, your health and wellbeing should be a priority! Boston is a city that knows exactly how important a healthy and sustainable lifestyle is, so whether you prefer walking, running, or biking, you’ll always see Bostonians on the move. So, f you’re an athletic person, this is one of the greatest advantages of living in Boston!

If you have a roommate who enjoys exercise and the outdoors, consider moving to Boston and coliving in an apartment with one another.

And be sure to break in your running shoes before you move!
