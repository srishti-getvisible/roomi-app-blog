Getting to know Myrtle Beach, South Carolina

Myrtle Beach is a coastal city located on the East Coast of the United States. In the center of a continuous 60-mile (97-km) stretch of beach known as “The Grand Strand” in northeastern South Carolina, lies Myrtle Beach.

Myrtle Beach experiences a humid subtropical climate. Summers are hot and humid, and winters are brief and stormy, and it is wet and partly cloudy year-round.

How Transit Friendly is Myrtle Beach, South Carolina?

The Waccamaw Coast Regional Transportation Authority (Coast RTA) is the primary public transportation system along the Coastal Carolina region (which includes Myrtle Beach, Surfside Beach, Conway, Loris, and Aynor). You can also get to Garden City, Murrells Inlet, Pawleys Island, Georgetown, and Andrews.
