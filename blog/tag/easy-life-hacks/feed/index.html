So, our fantastic team took it upon themselves to bring you some easy life hacks and apartment hacks that could make your life simpler and save you tons of chore time.

1. Easy life hack: Coffee cubes!

This one goes out to all the coffee lovers in the house! An easy hack to keep your cold brews deliciously cold and heavily caffeinated! So, the next time you think about making coffee, keep this easy life hack in mind!

Here’s a great recipe for Iced Mocha with Coffee Cubes!

2. Easy life hack with ketchup bottles

At Roomi, we are big on organizing a mess and keeping things tidy. So here’s a hack that lets you kill and beat those hard to reach dusty spots. Grab a squeezy ketchup bottle head and let the vacuuming commence. One of the most ingenious apartment hacks out there!

3. Easy life hack with paper clips!

Is your bedside table a pool of tangled writhing snake-y wires? Use this hack to organize your charging cables and save time on untangling before every use.

4. Easy life hack for your wires!

What is our life without extension cords and charging cables? Having said that, who likes to have random wires thrown across the room or having a perpetual wire across their bed? So, here’s how to pull the plug on your situation! Use empty toilet roles to wrap and neatly store cables for you and your roomi. Alternatively, add waste cardboard from amazon packages to create a box division for longer cables & chargers. This is the best apartment hack ever!

5. Magic vinegar

Got dysfunctional shower head? Here’s an easy life hack to unclog and make your showerheads functional again! You’re welcome! And trust us, this is one of the best apartment hacks out there!

6. Easy life hack for tiny shoes!

Shoes too tight? Well, this simple life hack will help you loosen ’em up for happy feet! Now, go and dance the night away in your ball gown!

7. Easy life hack for your candles!

Don’t burn your fingers because the Italians have saved the day again with pasta! Use uncooked spaghetti to light the wick. This is one of the most easy life hacks out there!

8. Spaghetti hack!

Speaking of spaghetti, do you ever struggle to store it properly? Here’s an easy life hack that’s canceled all the other boring ways to store spaghetti.

9. Plastic bottle hack!

Another one of those excellent apartment hacks that repurpose the big plastic bottles sitting under your sink. You could also paint and use the remaining plastic for potting a lovely plant!

10. Beach hack!

Let the beach take care of all your life troubles while this easy life hack takes care of all your “how will I keep my stuff waterproof on the beach?” troubles.

11. Microwave hack!

The world needed this hack!!! How many times does one have to stick their plate in the microwave to get their food hot and steamy? Our score is 3, how about you?

12. Toaster hack!

Sandwich lovers in the house? Let’s get toasted!

13. Nutella hack!

Okay, this one just speaks for itself. This easy life hack will Redefine your vanilla ice-cream experience at home like nothing else.

15. Dental Floss hack!

A neat way to divide the cake and other soft stuff! Especially when you have a roomi who always ends up taking the bigger piece. Easy life hack, FTW!

16. Doritos hack!

Well, this is an unexpected (And easy) life hack! Next time you go hiking or camping, take a bag of Doritos. Not only does it make a scrumptious snack, but it also doubles up as a campfire. Who knew?

17. Emergency Stash

Talking about hiking & traveling, here’s a nifty hack to store your emergency funds. This is an easy life hack that really comes in handy during emergencies!
