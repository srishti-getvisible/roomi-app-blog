Cultural Activities in Salem

It doesn’t matter if you’re interested in history or not. There’s no shortage of fun and educational activities for visitors in Salem. There’ll be something that appeals to everyone on this city guide for Salem. So, take a look at this list of cool things to do when you visit the Witch City!

1. Salem Witch Museum

The most popular attraction of the city, the Salem Witch Museum, claims to be one of America’s strangest. It seems like a morbid place for children… but children love it! There is a witch testimony room and an old-fashioned cemetery where they have built life-size statues of the victims of the Witch Trials.

2. Salem Willows

Whether your favorite form of entertainment is attending a concert or museum outing, the Salem Willows is the perfect location for enjoying an array of cultural activities. The lakeside park hosts concerts, children’s shows, and events. You will also see dance performances, arts and crafts festivals, and more here.

3. Peabody Essex Museum

The Peabody Essex Museum is a must-visit destination in the historic seaport city. Located in the heart of downtown, this museum showcases an impressive collection of art from around the world and focuses on preserving our nation’s maritime history.
