]]> Visiting the Fairfield County area and looking for a good meal? Look no further. Fairfield County is a hidden gem for quality restaurants. I mean, the sheer quality of the restaurants in CT will surprise any foodie. But the restaurants in Fairfield County, CT are a whole other deal. Whether you travel to Ridgefield or New Canaan, Fairfield County offers a wide range of delicious options that will suit every taste. From pizza joints to farm-to-table, there’s something for everyone in Fairfield County.

Here’s a list of 8 restaurants that are the best in the area.

1. Bailey’s Backyard

This restaurant is located in Ridgefield, CT, Bailey’s Backyard is a quaint farm-to-table restaurant. Quintly nestled right off of the main street, Bailey’s is a welcoming spot that will make you feel as if you are having an intimate meal in someone’s backyard. Offering a delectable seasonal menu packed with a variety of unique items, their pan-seared salmon and 50-50 burgers come highly recommended. Not only will you enjoy a delicious meal at Bailey’s, but you’ll also be supporting local farmers by dining at this sustainable farm-to-table restaurant. Win, win!

2. SE Uncorked

SE Uncorked is the perfect spot for a night of beer and burgers. This restaurant is located in New Canaan, CT, and is the perfect spot for anyone looking for unique pub food and a wide range of delicious cocktails, shots, beer, and wine too. Yes please! Just be sure to check out their double cheeseburger or UC cheesesteak. And we promise you won’t be disappointed.

3. Colony Grill

Colony Grill is a fan favorite for Fairfield residents. Located in Fairfield, CT, Colony Grill is a hip pizza joint that specializes in thin-crust pizza. This restaurant is super popular, so make sure to call ahead of time to make a reservation. Serving incredible thin-crust pizzas with a signature hot oil, you’re guaranteed to finish every bite.

4. Mecha Noodle Bar

Noodle lovers rejoice! Located in Stamford, CT, Mecha Noodle Bar offers a range of delectable Asian comfort foods. Ranging from pho and ramen to fried chicken, there’s something for everyone on Mecha’s menu. Grab a cocktail at the bar and enjoy a bowl of your favorite dish.

5. Bodega Taco Bar

Offering creative Mexican food, Bodega Taco Bar is located in Fairfield, CT, and we’re telling you – this joint is the perfect spot for any fiesta! The bar is equipped with a variety of 80+ tequilas and inventive cocktails… what’s not to love? Be sure to try their Crunch Kale and Mushroom taco while you’re there too. Go grab a margarita, tacos… and enjoy!

6. Barcelona Wine Bar

Barcelona Wine Bar offers an authentic Spanish dining experience shaped by Mediterranean influences. Located in Fairfield, CT, it’s certainly a dining hot spot. Begin the night with countless tapas and enjoy traditional plates such as paella and Spanish rice. Let’s just say, Barcelona Wine Club is bound to become your new favourite place to be.

7. Bartaco

Located in Westport, CT, Bartaco is a hip Mexican style restaurant. The restaurant’s ambiance is a mesh of Brazilian and Southern California culture, and your dining experience will make you feel as though you’ve stepped onto a breezy beach! Bartaco offers a range of dishes including specialty tacos and empanadas, not to mention their delicious range of cocktails and margaritas. Sit back, relax, and enjoy your tacos and cocktails with a beautiful view of Long Island Sound!

8. Hoodoo Brown BBQ

Hoodoo Brown BBQ is everyone’s favorite barbeque joint. Located in Ridgefield, CT, Hoodoo Brown’s BBQ is the perfect spot for satisfying and hearty, comfort food. Offering a range of classic barbeque food including pulled pork, brisket, and ribs, there’s a lot to choose from! Take your pick of cocktails, beers, and wines and get a real taste of Texas at Hoodoo Brown BBQ.

]]> One major lifestyle change that we all have had to adapt to is COVID-19’s effect on the restaurant dining experience. In the era of COVID-19, many restaurants are now offering more options for contactless delivery and takeout. Most restaurants are even beginning to open back up with socially distanced outdoor and indoor seating. Wearing masks when getting up out of your seat and using disposable or contactless menus at restaurants has now become the new normal. And if you’re living in NYC you’d know that the same restrictions apply to Manhattan restaurants too!

Despite these new safety COVID-19 precautions, restaurant dining can still be an enjoyable experience and a way to socialize with friends and family safely. Here are 5 delicious and reasonably priced restaurants in Manhattan to try that are open for takeout delivery as well as restaurant dining.

5 Best Manhattan Restaurants to Try For Takeout, Delivery & Restaurant Dining

1. Toloache

Mexican restaurant with multiple locations throughout NYC

Price: $$

Toloache is open for outdoor dining and takeout. Toloache offers brunch, lunch, and dinner. Toloache’s bar is open until midnight Friday – Saturday night. This restaurant also offers delivery through Caviar and Postmates. Check out their menu! Takeout, delivery, and restaurant dining all in one!

2. Harding’s

Upscale American restaurant located at 32 E 21st St.

Price: $$

Harding’s is open for takeout, delivery, and patio dining. Harding’s is open Tuesday – Sunday from 5:00pm – 10:00pm, offering a tasty dinner and drinks menu. This restaurant also offers home delivery through Grubhub.

3. Shuka

Eastern Mediterranean restaurant located at 38 MacDougal St.

Price: $$

Shuka is open for takeout, delivery, and restaurant dining – with brunch, lunch, and dinner available. This restaurant in Manhattan also offers delivery through Caviar. So takeout, delivery, and restaurant dining all in one!

4. Blue Ribbon Sushi

Sushi bar with multiple locations throughout NYC

Price: $$

Blue Ribbon Sushi offers takeout, delivery, and restaurant dining. Blue Ribbon has a variety of locations and bars throughout the city that are listed on their website. This restaurant in Manhattan also offers delivery through Caviar and Doordash. Another great option for takeout, delivery & restaurant dining.

5. The Smith

American restaurant with multiple locations throughout NYC

Price: $$

The Smith offers takeout, delivery, and restaurant dining. The Smith has locations in Nomad, Lincoln Square, Midtown, and the East Village, and offers brunch, lunch, and dinner.

5 Best Apps to Use for Restaurant Delivery

Want to have a night in with your favorite cuisine? Whether you’re not comfortable eating out at a restaurant just yet due to COVID-19, or you want to take a break from cooking and have a night in, here are 5 delivery apps that will bring you your favorite restaurant’s food right to your door.

1. DoorDash

DoorDash is available as an online platform and app. You can browse for food through filters, such as specific cuisines, restaurants near you, etc. Doordash also offers live tracking of your order.

2. Uber Eats

Uber Eats is available as an online platform and app. You can browse food through filters, such as food near you, healthy food, fast food, etc. Uber Eats also offers live tracking of your order.

3. Postmates

Postmates is available as an online platform and app, delivering food, drinks, groceries, and other grocery essential items to your door.

4. Grubhub

Grubhub is available as an online platform and app. You can browse food by cuisines, restaurants, or cities.

5. Caviar

This platform is available as an online platform and app. Caviar allows you to enter your location in order to find food near you.
