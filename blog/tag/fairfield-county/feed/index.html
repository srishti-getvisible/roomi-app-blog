]]> Considering moving to Fairfield County, CT? There are numerous reasons why so many people love this spot in Connecticut. With so many towns in Fairfield County all with their unique personality and small-town charm, there’s bound to be one that’s right for you! Not to mention Fairfield County is only an hour away from NYC, making it a hotspot for NYC commuters.

If you’re thinking about moving to Fairfield County but are on the fence, here are 5 reasons why Fairfield County is one of the best places to live!

1. It’s a short train ride away from NYC

One appealing quality of Fairfield County is that it’s only about an hour away from the city, making this spot in Connecticut an ideal home for NYC commuters. Many New Yorkers have been moving to Connecticut to replace the bustle of NYC with a small town that’s still a reasonable distance away.

Because of COVID-19, Connecticut is becoming even more attractive to New Yorkers. Many people are now looking for a way out of the crowded city and desire a quieter, more affordable area – since most people are now working from home anyways.

Many New Yorkers are looking to move to Fairfield County since living in a bustling city is no longer desirable because of COVID-19. Real estate prices are normally very high in here, however, they are now beginning to drop in order to make moving to the suburbs more desirable and affordable for New Yorkers. So, be sure to start your search now if you’re interested in moving here!

2. It’s home to delicious restaurants

One thing that many people don’t know about Fairfield County is that there are tons of delicious restaurants. From Mexican cuisine to pizza joints, there are loads of hidden gems here! Some of the best towns to check out for food are Westport, Fairfield, New Canaan, and Greenwich. A fan favorite for Fairfield locals is Colony Pizza. It won’t disappoint!

3. There are countless outdoor activities

Another great part about living here is the range of outdoor activities. Since it’s located on the coast of Connecticut on Long Island Sound, there are plenty of beaches to explore along the

In addition to beautiful beaches, Fairfield County is home to many hiking trails. No matter what town you live in, you’re bound to find a few hidden hiking gems!

4. The houses are stunning

One of the best aspects of Fairfield County is the architecture. Towns such as Fairfield, Westport, and Greenwich are home to stunning waterfront and beach homes. If you’re looking for a house that feels like a weekend getaway everyday, Fairfield County is the place for you!

If you’re looking for somewhere a little quieter, towns such as Redding, Wilton, and Ridgefield might be a great fit for you. Not only are these towns charming but they’re also filled with beautiful houses. From classic New England colonial inspired homes to multi-million dollar houses, there’s something to fit everyone’s taste in Fairfield County.

5. You get a choice of 23 cute towns

With 23 towns nestled in Fairfield County, there are countless options for the location of your new home. Each town has its own unique qualities and personality. For example, if you’re looking for a town with a small town center and a beach, Fairfield or Westport could be perfect for you. However, if you don’t want to pay for the price of a beach town, Bethel or Newtown might be a better option. Whatever town you choose, you’ll get all the benefits that Fairfield County has to offer!

]]> Visiting the Fairfield County area and looking for a good meal? Look no further. Fairfield County is a hidden gem for quality restaurants. I mean, the sheer quality of the restaurants in CT will surprise any foodie. But the restaurants in Fairfield County, CT are a whole other deal. Whether you travel to Ridgefield or New Canaan, Fairfield County offers a wide range of delicious options that will suit every taste. From pizza joints to farm-to-table, there’s something for everyone in Fairfield County.

Here’s a list of 8 restaurants that are the best in the area.

1. Bailey’s Backyard

This restaurant is located in Ridgefield, CT, Bailey’s Backyard is a quaint farm-to-table restaurant. Quintly nestled right off of the main street, Bailey’s is a welcoming spot that will make you feel as if you are having an intimate meal in someone’s backyard. Offering a delectable seasonal menu packed with a variety of unique items, their pan-seared salmon and 50-50 burgers come highly recommended. Not only will you enjoy a delicious meal at Bailey’s, but you’ll also be supporting local farmers by dining at this sustainable farm-to-table restaurant. Win, win!

2. SE Uncorked

SE Uncorked is the perfect spot for a night of beer and burgers. This restaurant is located in New Canaan, CT, and is the perfect spot for anyone looking for unique pub food and a wide range of delicious cocktails, shots, beer, and wine too. Yes please! Just be sure to check out their double cheeseburger or UC cheesesteak. And we promise you won’t be disappointed.

3. Colony Grill

Colony Grill is a fan favorite for Fairfield residents. Located in Fairfield, CT, Colony Grill is a hip pizza joint that specializes in thin-crust pizza. This restaurant is super popular, so make sure to call ahead of time to make a reservation. Serving incredible thin-crust pizzas with a signature hot oil, you’re guaranteed to finish every bite.

4. Mecha Noodle Bar

Noodle lovers rejoice! Located in Stamford, CT, Mecha Noodle Bar offers a range of delectable Asian comfort foods. Ranging from pho and ramen to fried chicken, there’s something for everyone on Mecha’s menu. Grab a cocktail at the bar and enjoy a bowl of your favorite dish.

5. Bodega Taco Bar

Offering creative Mexican food, Bodega Taco Bar is located in Fairfield, CT, and we’re telling you – this joint is the perfect spot for any fiesta! The bar is equipped with a variety of 80+ tequilas and inventive cocktails… what’s not to love? Be sure to try their Crunch Kale and Mushroom taco while you’re there too. Go grab a margarita, tacos… and enjoy!

6. Barcelona Wine Bar

Barcelona Wine Bar offers an authentic Spanish dining experience shaped by Mediterranean influences. Located in Fairfield, CT, it’s certainly a dining hot spot. Begin the night with countless tapas and enjoy traditional plates such as paella and Spanish rice. Let’s just say, Barcelona Wine Club is bound to become your new favourite place to be.

7. Bartaco

Located in Westport, CT, Bartaco is a hip Mexican style restaurant. The restaurant’s ambiance is a mesh of Brazilian and Southern California culture, and your dining experience will make you feel as though you’ve stepped onto a breezy beach! Bartaco offers a range of dishes including specialty tacos and empanadas, not to mention their delicious range of cocktails and margaritas. Sit back, relax, and enjoy your tacos and cocktails with a beautiful view of Long Island Sound!

8. Hoodoo Brown BBQ

Hoodoo Brown BBQ is everyone’s favorite barbeque joint. Located in Ridgefield, CT, Hoodoo Brown’s BBQ is the perfect spot for satisfying and hearty, comfort food. Offering a range of classic barbeque food including pulled pork, brisket, and ribs, there’s a lot to choose from! Take your pick of cocktails, beers, and wines and get a real taste of Texas at Hoodoo Brown BBQ.
