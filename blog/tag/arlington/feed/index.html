There’s a lack of easy public transportation available in Arlington. According to the News Archives’ in 1980, 1995 and 2002, Arlington residents voted down mass transit options. However, we have compiled a list of transit options in Arlington that will make your commute and life easier!

Arlington rates 82 on walkability and most errands can be accomplished on foot.

Taxis are easily available and you can call 24/7. Most accept cash and card payment for services.
