]]> Characterized by glamour and grandeur, Beverly Hills is a famous city in Los Angeles county that is filled with Hollywood history. Beverly Hills is actually built on mostly flat land and is unofficially divided into North and South by Santa Monica Boulevard. It is a small suburb with just over 34,000 residents, but life in Beverly Hills is affluent and complex. This neighborhood is home to some of the best bars and restaurants in the United States. Read on to find out some great bars and pubs in Beverly Hills you can try.

1. The Honor Bar

The Honor Bar is a casual and sleek bar & grill that serves a great selection of first rate cocktails, wine and beers. If you’re hungry in between drinks, try their tasty snacks, sandwiches and hand-rolled sushi. This bar, though casual, encourages its customers to avoid wearing clothes that are too casual, such as tank tops, flip flops, and athletic attire.

Timings: Sun – Thu : 11:30am – 10pm Fri – Sat : 11:30am – 11pm

2. Heritage Fine Wines

This bar in Beverly Hills is a mix of an authentic French restaurant and wine bar that serves organic, biodynamic, natural, and high-quality French wines. It is home to a cool piano bar and provides live music entertainment at night. It also serves fine French gourmet products like caviar, fine tea, and more. Prepare yourself for the ultimate dining experience.

Timings: Monday: Closed Tue-Sat: 11am – 11pm Sunday: Closed

WEBSITE

3. The Bar Room

Adjacent to another great restaurant/bar (Porta Via), the Bar Room is ready to serve its customers classic cocktails, wine, and beer, as well as a variety of small plates. If you’re looking to unwind after a long, hard day, then head on down to the Bar Room and relax in their movable leather banquettes while sipping on old-time cocktails.

Timings: Mon-Sun: 10:00a.m –9:00p.m Tue-Thur: 10:00a.m – 9:30p.m Fri-Sat: 10:00a.m – 10:00p.m

WEBSITE

4. THEBlvd Restaurant

Located at Beverly Wilshire Hotel, this restaurant and lounge in Beverly Hills with great interior decor and outside seats, serves fresh Californian cuisine and offers a wide selection of wine, beer, cocktails and Ruinart champagne. THEBlvd is also known for crafting unique and fanciful cocktails. Or, pair your meals with a refreshing glass of rose while enjoying views of the famous Rodeo Drive.

Timings: Mon–Sun: 2:30pm to 6:00pm

WEBSITE

5. The Roof Garden at the Peninsula

Whether you’re a hotel guest at The Peninsula hotels or a local resident, you can enjoy the services at the Roof Garden. This chic restaurant with its outdoor pool and spa serves fresh Californian cuisine and provides a robust wine menu at the lounge. Around the restaurant’s fire pit, you can enjoy your choice of wine, champagne or cocktails, perfect for warm-weather sipping.

Timings: Every day, 7:00 a.m – 9:00 p.m

WEBSITE

6. 3rd Stop

This is a great place to stop if you’re craving a cold beer late at night. The 3rd Stop is a charming beer & wine bar that serves New American bites & meals. In this restaurant, you will also find ample booths & several TVs. The bar offers a menu of local, craft-focused beer on 36 regularly rotated taps. Wine is available on tap by the glass or carafe. So if you’re in the mood for a chill night out, then this is the place to be.

Timings: MON – FRI: 11:30 AM – 11 PM SAT & SUN: 4 PM – 11 PM

WEBSITE

7. Ocean Prime

This award-winning modern American pub in Beverly Hills buzzes with radiant energy and provides an impressive menu of seafood and steaks. It’s a great place to socialize and indulge. The team serves excellent cocktails and you can be assured of good customer service.

Timings: Mon-Thurs: 11:30am-10:00pm Fri : 11:30am- 11:00pm Sat: 4:00am- 11:00pm Sun: 4:00am – 9:00pm

8. Maybourne Bar

You can always look forward to a vibrant night when you visit the Maybourne Bar in Beverly Hills. You’ll find an exceptional wine list and a cocktail menu filled with classic and new creations, all handcrafted by bartenders with the freshest ingredients and finest spirits.

Timings: Daily: 4pm – 12am

WEBSITE

9. Avec Nous

Oo la la! Experience French elegance and glamour in Avec Nous (translates to ‘with us’ in English). Avec Nous is a contemporary classic French bistro that gives off a hip St. Tropez vibe in Beverly Hills. It is located in the Viceroy L’Ermitage hotel in Beverly Hills, and provides excellent French cuisine with an extensive wine list.

Timings: Daily : 8:00 a.m to 10:00 p.m

WEBSITE

10. Bar Noir

This fashionable cocktail lounge in the Maison 140 hotel is one of the best bars in Beverly Hills. It comes with a rich interior and comfortable seating and gives off an intimate feel. It’s a very cozy spot for drinks, with some pretty cool bartenders!

Timings: Mon-Sat: 5 a.m – 11 p.m Sunday: Closed

WEBSITE

11. The Roof at EDITION

Rooftop bars are perfect for soaking up views for a great dining experience. This is exactly why The Roof at EDITION is one of the elite bars in Beverly Hills. Accompanied with wooden bench seating, mature trees and terra cotta potted plants, this restaurant offers a menu with Latin and tropical-inspired cocktails with Southern California flair. From tequila to wellness cocktails, you’ll be spoilt for choice in this bar.

Timings: Sun-Wed: 4:00pm – 10:00pm Thur-Sat: 4:00pm – 11:00pm

WEBSITE

12. Mastro’s Restaurant

A masterful and high-end restaurant and bar in Beverly Hills that serves decadent plates & drinks to glamorous crowds in a plush supper-club setting. It has been acclaimed by critics and customers alike.

Timings: Sun-Sat: 5:00pm – 10:00pm

WEBSITE

13. CUT Lounge

Located in a four-seasons hotel, the CUT Lounge is an ideal place to unwind after a long day. Guests can enjoy craft cocktails and snacks in a warm and sophisticated environment.

Timings: Mon- Sat: 5pm – 1:30am Closed Sunday

WEBSITE

14. The Roof on Wilshire

This lovely rooftop bar provides a full view of the city and is decorated with comfy lounge chairs and cabanas. They serve classic American dishes and drinks spanning from daytime carafes of mimosas and bloody Mary, to nighttime signature cocktails, selected wines, and beer on tap.

Timings: Mon-Fri: 7:00 a.m to 11 p.m
Sat & Sun: 11:00 a.m to 11:00 p.m
