]]> Considering moving to Fairfield County, CT? There are numerous reasons why so many people love this spot in Connecticut. With so many towns in Fairfield County all with their unique personality and small-town charm, there’s bound to be one that’s right for you! Not to mention Fairfield County is only an hour away from NYC, making it a hotspot for NYC commuters.

If you’re thinking about moving to Fairfield County but are on the fence, here are 5 reasons why Fairfield County is one of the best places to live!

1. It’s a short train ride away from NYC

One appealing quality of Fairfield County is that it’s only about an hour away from the city, making this spot in Connecticut an ideal home for NYC commuters. Many New Yorkers have been moving to Connecticut to replace the bustle of NYC with a small town that’s still a reasonable distance away.

Because of COVID-19, Connecticut is becoming even more attractive to New Yorkers. Many people are now looking for a way out of the crowded city and desire a quieter, more affordable area – since most people are now working from home anyways.

Many New Yorkers are looking to move to Fairfield County since living in a bustling city is no longer desirable because of COVID-19. Real estate prices are normally very high in here, however, they are now beginning to drop in order to make moving to the suburbs more desirable and affordable for New Yorkers. So, be sure to start your search now if you’re interested in moving here!

2. It’s home to delicious restaurants

One thing that many people don’t know about Fairfield County is that there are tons of delicious restaurants. From Mexican cuisine to pizza joints, there are loads of hidden gems here! Some of the best towns to check out for food are Westport, Fairfield, New Canaan, and Greenwich. A fan favorite for Fairfield locals is Colony Pizza. It won’t disappoint!

3. There are countless outdoor activities

Another great part about living here is the range of outdoor activities. Since it’s located on the coast of Connecticut on Long Island Sound, there are plenty of beaches to explore along the

In addition to beautiful beaches, Fairfield County is home to many hiking trails. No matter what town you live in, you’re bound to find a few hidden hiking gems!

4. The houses are stunning

One of the best aspects of Fairfield County is the architecture. Towns such as Fairfield, Westport, and Greenwich are home to stunning waterfront and beach homes. If you’re looking for a house that feels like a weekend getaway everyday, Fairfield County is the place for you!

If you’re looking for somewhere a little quieter, towns such as Redding, Wilton, and Ridgefield might be a great fit for you. Not only are these towns charming but they’re also filled with beautiful houses. From classic New England colonial inspired homes to multi-million dollar houses, there’s something to fit everyone’s taste in Fairfield County.

5. You get a choice of 23 cute towns

With 23 towns nestled in Fairfield County, there are countless options for the location of your new home. Each town has its own unique qualities and personality. For example, if you’re looking for a town with a small town center and a beach, Fairfield or Westport could be perfect for you. However, if you don’t want to pay for the price of a beach town, Bethel or Newtown might be a better option. Whatever town you choose, you’ll get all the benefits that Fairfield County has to offer!

]]> When you’re a 20-something, there’s no better place to live than The Big Apple! New York City is full of countless opportunities and experiences that are perfect for anyone in their 20s.

If you’ve recently graduated college and are looking for a fun and hip place to live, consider finding an apartment in NYC! Deciding on a place to live after graduating college can be daunting, but it doesn’t have to be.

Here are a few reasons why New York City is one of the best places to live in your 20s. You won’t regret it!

1. There are countless career opportunities

If you’re unsure of what job you want and want to explore different career options, NYC is the place to be. The city is full of countless career opportunities and possibilities, and salaries are often higher in NYC! If you’re looking for a career in business or communications, the perks of living in New York in your 20s are that a huge range of successful companies from different industries are located in the city.

For those interested in diving into the real estate industry, New York City offers numerous opportunities. Whether you’re looking to transition to a new career or expand your skills, online real estate courses can provide the flexibility to learn at your own pace while experiencing city life. These courses are perfect for young professionals aiming to secure their future without sacrificing their bustling lifestyle.

Since there are many opportunities, there’s also loads of competition, so be sure to bring your A-game to every interview.

2. Learn how to become more independent

Living in NYC is a great way to learn how to become more independent. Even though college and graduate school teach independence, living on your own for the first time is the number one way to learn how to function by yourself. NYC can be a tough place to live, but you’ll soon learn how to handle some of life’s most difficult challenges and grow tougher skin in the process.

Moving to a bustling city on your own can be scary and extremely rewarding at the same time. You’ll take on new responsibilities such as learning how to budget your money and figuring out how to pay your rent on time.

One thing is for sure, the perks of living in New York in your 20s is that it’ll definitely teach you what it’s like to live in the real world!

3. There’s always something to do in New York

One fantastic aspect of the city is that you’ll never be bored! NYC is home to countless exciting activities to do on the weekends. From breathtaking museums to exhilarating nightlife, there’s always something to do no matter what time of day it is.

Are you an art lover? Pay a visit to the Metropolitan Museum of Art or the Guggenheim. Are you a foodie? NYC has no shortage of restaurants. From Italian to Mediterranean cuisine, there’s something for everyone in NYC.

New York is truly the city that never sleeps!

4. You’ll meet lots of new people living in NYC

There’s nothing better than expanding your social circle when you move to a new place. New York City is an extremely diverse living area and home to over 8 million people. While living in NYC in your 20s, your horizons will broaden and you’ll be exposed to new food, cultures, and ideas.

One great way to get introduced to new people is to find a roommate in NYC. Not only is co-living with a roommate a financially smart decision, but it will also give you a confidant to help you navigate your new city!

5. You’ll gain a better understanding of yourself

Living in New York will help you to gain more confidence in yourself. Between the constant activity and new responsibilities, you’re bound to grow up quickly within your first year of living in NYC.

That being said, NYC is one of the best places to discover yourself in. While living in NYC in your 20s, you’ll be surrounded by new art, culture, food, and people. There’s so much to explore in the city, and being a 20-something is the perfect time to embrace it all!
